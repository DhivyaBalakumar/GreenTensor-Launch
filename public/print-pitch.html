// Why Now — The Regulatory Tailwind
The EU AI Act and SEC climate disclosure rules are forcing every company to measure and report AI emissions — starting now. AI compute costs are exploding. A single GPT-4 training run emits ~500 tonnes of CO₂. Yet no standard tooling exists for ML teams. GreenTensor is the only tool that makes this automatic — dropping directly into existing PyTorch workflows with a single context manager. We're not building for the future. We're solving a problem that exists today.
train.py — 3 lines to instrument
from greentensor import GreenTensor

# Wrap your existing training loop
with GreenTensor() as gt:
  with gt.mixed_precision():
    train()  # your code, unchanged

# That's it. Carbon, water, threats,
# ESG — all tracked automatically.
